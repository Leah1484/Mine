Most of the challenges regarding reproductive health that women across the world face are due to lack of access to sufficient education on the topic. Some of these challenges include: early pregnancy and parenthood, difficulties accessing contraception, and high rates of HIV and sexually transmitted infections. I got my inspiration to educate women on issues affecting their reproductive health after identifying the gap in knowledge between the health care providers and the clients. I endeavor to deliver reproductive health awareness in social spaces mostly the churches, welfare groups and schools.

What I do

Reproductive Health Talks

During these sessions we discuss in detail key areas that are related to reproduction. Some of the topics discussed are Contraceptives, Pregnancy, Health concerns related to Reproductive organs, Sex and many more.

Consultations

Being a nurse by profession, I offer consultation services on any health related matters. If you are in an emergency and there's no medical practitioner nearby, please feel free to reach out.

Volunteering

I often volunteer in various locations especially those in remote locations. I volunteer with organizations such as Rock-health integrated community organization (RICO) where we aim at deliverying primary health care services in the remote areas.
